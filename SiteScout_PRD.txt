Product Requirements Document (PRD)
Product Name:  NoSiteFinder – SaaS Lead‑Gen Tool for Businesses Without Websites\ Version:  0.9 (Draft)\
Author:  ChatGPT (Assistant)\ Date:  July 7, 2025
1  Overview
1.1  Problem Statement
More than 25 % of small local businesses in the U.S. still operate without a dedicated website, missing out
on search visibility, credibility, and online revenue. Web‑design agencies waste hours manually prospecting
these businesses.
1.2  Solution Statement
NoSiteFinder automatically surfaces businesses that lack a website, enriches each lead with contact data,
and equips agencies with AI‑generated marketing copy and one‑click CSV export—shrinking prospecting
time from hours to minutes.
1.3  Strategic Fit
Vision:  Empower every local business to get online.
Mission:  Become the go‑to lead‑gen platform for digital marketers and web studios worldwide.
Competitive Advantage:  Niche focus, real‑time data, built‑in AI copy, friction‑less UI.

5  Functional Requirements
5.1  Business Discovery & Filtering
FR‑1 : Accept location (auto‑complete) & business category.
FR‑2 : Fetch businesses via Google Places API (and fallback providers).
FR‑3 : Detect existing website via website  field, MX records, and SERP check. Exclude matches.
5.2  Lead Table UI
FR‑4 : Infinite scroll + sticky column headers.
FR‑5 : Columns: Name, Category, Address, Phone, Website (blank), Rating, Reviews, Last Updated.
FR‑6 : Multi‑select rows, bulk actions (Export, Generate Copy).
5.3  AI Tagline & Email Generator
FR‑7 : For ≤ 200 selected rows, call LLM (Gemini 2.0 via Vertex AI) with templated prompt.
FR‑8 : Display taglines inline; allow copy‑to‑clipboard and .csv export.1.

7  Data & APIs
Primary Sources:  Google Places API, Yelp Fusion (backup), OpenStreetMap for geocoding.
Enrichment:  Clearbit Phone & domain status check.
Database:  PostgreSQL (RDS) + PostGIS; Redis cache for recent searches.
LLM:  Google Gemini 2.0 via Vertex AI.
